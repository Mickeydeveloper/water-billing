Billing Information & Instructions

Water Tariff Rates:

  • Standard Rate: TZS 2000/unit
  • Fixed Charge: TZS 0

How to Calculate Your Bill:

  1. Enter your previous meter reading
  2. Enter your current meter reading
  3. Set the rate per unit (if different from default)
  4. Add any fixed charges if applicable
  5. Click "Calculate" to get your bill
  6. Send the bill via SMS or view billing history

Formula:
Total Bill = (Current - Previous) × Rate/unit + Fixed Charge
